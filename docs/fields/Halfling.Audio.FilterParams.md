## FilterType
`enum FilterType` · optional · one of: `LowPass`, `LowPassAmplify`, `HighPass`, `HighPassAmplify`, `BandPassAmplify`, `Notch`

Specifies whether and how frequencies of audio will be filtered.

## Frequency
`int` · optional

Specifies the cutoff frequency used by the filter.

## QFactor
`float` · optional

Specifies the Q factor used by the filter. This value controls how rapidly the audio will be reduced. It is not used by the LowPass or HighPass filters.
