## NormalSound
`ISoundEffect` · optional

The sound to play when the input state reverts to its "Normal" state, or null to play no sound.

## HighlightedSound
`ISoundEffect` · optional

The sound to play when the input state switches from "Normal" to "Highlighted", or null to play no sound.

## PressedSound
`ISoundEffect` · optional

The sound to play when the input state switches to "Pressed" or "HotkeyPressed", or null to play no sound.

## AudioGroup
`AudioGroup` · optional

The AudioGroup in which the sounds will be played. If null, the GuiRoot's DefaultAudioGroup will be used.
