## ClipPadding
`Borders` · optional

The distances from the widget's borders outside of which children will not be rendered (assuming ClipChildren is true).

## BorderPadding
`Borders` · optional

The distances from the widget's ClipScreenRect that together define the client rectangle in which children are contained. The border padding is cumulative with the clip padding.

## CullChildren
`bool` · optional

Whether all out-of-view children are culled so that they will not be rendered.

## ClipChildren
`bool` · optional

Whether any pixels outside of the ClientScreenRect will be clipped (not rendered).

## ChildrenForceParentRendering
`bool` · optional

Whether any children whose rendering is being forced will also force their parent to render.
