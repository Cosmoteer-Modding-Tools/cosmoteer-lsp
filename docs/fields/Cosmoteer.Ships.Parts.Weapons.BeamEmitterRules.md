## SecondaryWidth
`number` · optional

The width of the beam's secondary collision check. The closer hit of the two checks is used for effects, allowing for 'proximity' hits with a more narrow criteria.

## SecondaryFilter
`EffectFilter` · optional

The filter used for the beam's secondary collision check, if SecondaryWidth is greater than 0.

## SecondaryOverrideFilter
`EffectFilter` · optional

The filter, if any, that the primary collision result is checked against in order to abort the secondary collision check.

## AntiPhasingRaycastDistance
`float` · optional

The distance backwards that the beam raycast is extended to help detect fixtures overlapping the emitter. Collisions detected within this distance will register at the beam emission location.

## ColliderOverlapTest
`bool` · optional
